Costi Survival World 12 New

Costi Survival World 12 is finally here! This is the first world in the series to feature a java modpack. Some of these mods include Create, Aether, Twilight Forest, Thermal Expansion, furniture packs, and more! This world will look entirely different from our previous worlds, as we may need to space ourselves out a little more. Thankfully, we also will have locomotive and teleportation travel methods.

Costi Survival World 11 / X will still be hosted on Costi Online Services and will continue running for the foreseeable future. For more information about Costi Survival World X, 11, and 12, please click the learn more button.
