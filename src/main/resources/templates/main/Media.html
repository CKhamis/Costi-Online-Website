What is Costi Media?

Costi Media is an alias I use to label my digital artwork such as videos and vector art. Costi Media went by 2 other names in the past, Costi Inc, and Costi Entertainment. Learn more about the rich history of Costi Media by viewing the timeline.

Costi logo History

View the history of my logos used for internal and external use

Personal Logo

2011 to present

My logo has existed for well over a decade. Conceptualized around 2010, it was inspired by a friend's logo, which was inspired by the Nintendo GameCube logo. Over the years, I have consistently used my logo for personal projects, internal documentation, and publicly facing projects (like this website). Like other vector based artwork I made the time, the first and second logos were made using Macromedia Fireworks 8. This was because I first learned how to use it for my multimedia 1 class in middle school. Eventually, I was given the Adobe CS4 and CS5 suite by my aunt and created my third logo. In High School, I found a way to get the full Adobe Suite for $20 a year, and since Fireworks was discontinued by Adobe, I began to use Adobe Illustrator, which I still use to this day. The 4th Logo was used in my work from 2016 to 2018, until I invented my most recent logo. My newest logo is a lot easier to consume, dense, and aesthetically coherent.

Age Logos

2016 to present

Since I was 15, I decided to create a logo every year representing me. This is so I can attach them to certain achievements or projects I have done. These are not generally publicly facing and are not used much in general. I use them mainly as an exercise to try making something new

Costi Media Logos

2018 to present

The first normal, non-animated logo used for Costi Media/Entertainment was created in 2018. It was made because I needed a logo for my YouTube channel which hosted the Ethan Show. You can still see the first logo displayed in the outro of the Ethan Show Episode 3. The second logo, "E", was created in the same year, along with my new personal logo. This was only used for one project and was not in any videos I made. In 2019, I created the "M" logo and renamed the YouTube channel to Costi Media. This was because the video content I was making at the time was more professional and not only for entertainment. Finally, in 2020, I re-did the logo again in 2020 because I thought the previous one was too plain and unrecognizable.
