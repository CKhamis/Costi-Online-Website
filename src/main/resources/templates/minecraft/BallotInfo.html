Overview

Elections are run every 3 months, for each election there will be a ballot. Complete the ballot before the deadline to have your vote count.

Candidate Elections

Candidates are only allowed to run for mayoral positions or lower (if the mayor decides to create more positions). You will be seeing their statements and issues on this page. Mayors can only govern one city at a time. Chose who you think would be the best fit for mayor.

Referendums

Referendums are laws that will be passed with the majority of a city/ world's vote. Mayors can add referendums to the ballot by asking Costi. Mayors are not required to pass laws through a referendum.
